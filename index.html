Book Designs

I have worked on various book design projects, focusing on both visual storytelling and thoughtful typography. My experience includes designing covers, layouts, and visual identities for fiction, non-fiction, and art books. Each project is tailored to reflect the tone and message of the content, blending aesthetics with readability.

Magazines

In my magazine design projects, I explored ways to reflect the personalities and stories of the people featured. Working closely with thoughtful, sensitive individuals, I aimed to create layouts and visual narratives that conveyed their depth and character. The design process was guided by subtlety, emotion, and a desire to translate human nuances into form.

Posters

My poster projects range from digital compositions made in Adobe to handcrafted collages sewn together from paper fragments. Each poster was an experiment in form and material — from clean graphic design to tactile still lifes. This variety allowed me to explore different visual languages and turn simple ideas into expressive statements.

Collaborations

I’ve collaborated with creatives from various fields — including fashion, fine art, and graphic design — working together on interdisciplinary projects that combined different perspectives and skills. These collaborations enriched my approach and taught me to adapt within diverse teams. Unfortunately, not all works are available for sharing due to prior agreements.
